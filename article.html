615/422 Collins Street Melbourne

Freshly updated & perfectly positioned

Freshly painted, new blinds, new light fittings and appliances included! Secure this beautifully presented, one bedroom apartment situated in the heart of Melbourne's CBD. This updated apartment offers open plan living, modern kitchen including gas cooking and stainless steel appliances, large bedroom with built in mirrored robes and a spacious bathroom with European laundry facilities (washing machine & dryer included). This property boasts character with floor to ceiling windows allowing an abundance of natural light and high ceilings adding to the historical feel. You will enjoy easy access to the very best of Melbourne`s entertainment and culinary experiences. Within the free tram zone and a short walk to Flinders St train station the rest of the city is at your command.
